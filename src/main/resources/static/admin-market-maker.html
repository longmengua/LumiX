Market Maker Quick Guide

Follow these steps to create strategy limits, publish quote ladders, and inspect hedge status.

1. Save a market-maker strategy

Enter the market-maker ID, UID, symbol, notional limits, slippage limit, and kill switch state. Save Strategy persists the profile and the per-symbol risk limit.
